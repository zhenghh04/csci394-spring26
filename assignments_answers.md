### Reading Assignment for March 20, 2026

This Canvas item replaced the in-person class meeting while the instructor was out of town. Students were asked to read Chapter 23, "Computing in the Future: A Personal Perspective"; the chapter PDF was provided on Canvas for class use only and is not included in this repo.

The submission was a Word or PDF document due Friday, March 20, 2026 at midnight. Students needed to answer at least two of the prompt questions in 1-2 paragraphs each: the three-world model of HPC, limits of the von Neumann model for future HPC, cloud computing as a business model rather than a new technology, the problem addressed by memory-centric computing, and the theoretical power and practical difficulty of quantum computing.
